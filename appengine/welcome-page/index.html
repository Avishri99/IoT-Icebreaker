Get started with our IoT developer prototyping kit

Google has worked with Seeed Studio and BeagleBoard.org to put together a Developer prototyping kit based on the new Seeed Studio BeagleBone Green Wireless board.

This board includes

  • New improvements to the original BeagleBone Green, including built in wi-fi and bluetooth radios
  • A fully Open Hardware design
  • Built in Grove connectors, allow for prototyping without the need for soldering or complex breadboard work.
  • Built in onboard flash - lets you use SD card as fully removable
  • Built in PRU real-time co-processor - well suited for certain industrial protocols
  • Built in analog to digital conversion - key for many IoT prototyping situations
