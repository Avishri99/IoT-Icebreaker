Let's get started!

So you have your IoT prototyping Kit in front of you. Lets get it connected and run through a quick demo.

Connect to the Network

The Kit is equipped with the Seeed Studio BeagleBone Green wireless board. Follow these steps to get it connected

  • Connect the micro USB cable to the board and to a USB power supply - this can be your computer or a USB charger
  • After a minute or so - you should see a BeagleBoneNNNNNN network appear on your list of available wifi networks
  • Connect to this wifi network with your mobile phone and you should see a network provisioning screen come up
  • If you are connecting via a laptop, connect to this provisioning screen directly at http://192.168.8.1/login/
  • Choose to connect to your local network by choosing an SSID and specifying a password, your board should connect and display the IP address of your board on the local network - make a note of it
  • As currently configured you can now browse the local on-board BeagleBone documentaion - but you can click cancel (on your phone) or close and reconnect to your main wireless (if on a computer)

Your board shoud now be connected

Connect to the demo

The demo experience we'll get started with runs a small server on your board - we want to connect to that now. Your board should be reachable over a local multicast DNS name of "beaglebone.local" - but if that does not work, try the IP address you noted earlier.
